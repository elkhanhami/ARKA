Nature's Art in Architecture

A green wall is a living system that vertically cultivates plants on walls and building surfaces. This technology not only enhances the aesthetics of the environment but also provides numerous ecological benefits.

✓
Air Quality Improvement: CO2 absorption and oxygen production
✓
Noise Pollution Reduction: Natural sound insulation
✓
Energy Saving: Reduced heating and cooling consumption

About Green Walls

Green walls are not just about beauty; they are intelligent solutions for better living in cities.

Air Quality Improvement

Green walls improve air quality by up to 40% by absorbing carbon dioxide and producing oxygen.

  • Absorption of air pollutants
  • Production of fresh oxygen

Temperature Regulation

Green walls act as natural insulation, regulating ambient temperature by up to 5°C.

  • Reduced energy consumption
  • Natural insulation

Noise Pollution Reduction

The plant layer of green walls can reduce environmental noise pollution by up to 40 decibels.

  • Sound wave absorption
  • Creating environmental tranquility

Mental Health Benefits

Contact with nature in work and living environments reduces stress and increases productivity.

  • Stress reduction
  • Increased creativity

Types of Green Walls

Each system has its own advantages and applications. Choose according to your space.

Hydroponic System

Soil-less cultivation with smart drip irrigation. Suitable for indoor spaces and controlled environments.

Modular System

Pre-fabricated panels that allow for quick installation and easy replacement.

Cable System

Suitable for climbing plants and walls with organic, natural designs.

Fabric System

Lightweight and flexible with the ability to create curved and organic designs.

01

Consultation & Analysis

02

Conceptual Design
